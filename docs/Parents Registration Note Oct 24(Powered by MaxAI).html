Ballincollig 49th Scout Group
2024/25 Annual Registration Fees
Dear Parent/Guardian,
Firstly, we hope you and your family are doing well, now that Summer is over, and school is
well underway!
Were glad to report that all of our Beaver, Cub, Scout and Venture sections are back in full
swing since September and are now busy planning their scouting activities for the coming
year. A special welcome to any new parents whose children have recently joined.
With that in mind, wed like to let you know the plan for annual registration fees.
Particularly for any new parents, these fees cover a combination of:
- Payment to Scouting Ireland for services it provides nationally to all Scout Groups
- Funding retained by the Group to cover its ongoing running costs
- As part of our ongoing Group Development Plan, we are continuing to invest in the
Groups Facilities & Equipment and in training & developing our leaders. This enables
them to provide the best possible scouting experience for our youth members.
Were pleased to confirm that the existing registration fee below will be retained for the
coming year.
Inevitably, our Group also needs to carry out extra fundraising each year and we will be
communicating with you further over 2024/2025 on our plans and how you can help.
With above in mind, we are proposing the following fee structure, payable by Friday 31st
October 2024 via our Way2Pay text online link system :
- 195 for First Child
- 180 for Second Child
- 165 for Third Child
As always, please contact us should you need to alter how these payments are made. We
are committed to providing a quality scouting experience for all of our youth members.
Regards,
Ballincollig Scout Group
